- The objective of the game is to to be the first to remove all pieces off the board.
- Player 1 moves their pieces clockwise, Player 2 anti-clockwise.
- Pieces are moved in relation to dice rolls.
- Pieces on the bar must be removed before moving board pieces
- A piece cannot be moved to another spike if it has the other players pieces on
unless there is only 1 piece.
- Pieces can be cleared once they are all within your quarter of the board.
